Truly a Marvel of Evolution

Consider the giraffe.

Legs like stilts, neck like a grain elevator, shoulders like Atlas, the ass of a middle-aged man, head basically like a dragon (complete with strange little horns) and a tongue like H.R. Giger's vision of cunnilingus.

That's all. I was just thinking about giraffes.
